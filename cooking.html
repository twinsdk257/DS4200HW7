Foods I Enjoy Cooking

I enjoy cooking because its rewarding when you get to enjoy the results immediately after finishing the meal. During the weekday or late at night I prefer to cook more simple dishes that require very little prep or dishes. But on weekends I have more time to spend making more complicated foods like baked goods. So far I have made brownies, muffins, cheesecake, and a really bad macaron attempt.

I find preparing ingredients, seasoning food, and experimenting with flavors to be a fun and very satisfying process. Over time I have learned how to make several dishes that I enjoy and that my family also like on the occasion I make them. I feel like improving my cooking skills will only continue to be useful in the future after I am wrapped up with college
