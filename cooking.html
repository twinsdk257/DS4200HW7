Foods I Enjoy Cooking

Cooking has become one of my favorite hobbies because it allows me to be creative while also learning useful skills. I enjoy trying new recipes and improving my cooking techniques whenever I have time. Cooking is also rewarding because you get to enjoy the results immediately after finishing the meal. Sometimes I cook simple dishes during the week when I am busy with school, but on weekends I like to spend more time making something new or more complicated.

Another reason I enjoy cooking is because it helps me relax and focus on something different from academics. Preparing ingredients, seasoning food, and experimenting with flavors can be a fun and satisfying process. Over time I have learned how to make several dishes that I enjoy and that my friends also like when I cook for them. I plan to continue improving my cooking skills and trying new recipes whenever I have the chance.

Some Foods I Like to Cook

  • Pasta with tomato sauce
  • Fried rice with vegetables
  • Grilled steak
  • Chicken stir fry
  • Homemade ramen
