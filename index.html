About Me

My name is Sean Kim and I am currently studying Data Science and Environmental and Sustainability Sciences at Northeastern University. One of my favorite hobbies outside of school is cooking. Cooking has always been something I enjoy because when I get hungry I don't have to bother my parents and make something suited to my tastes. After a long day studying or being at school, cooking always helped me relax.

Cooking is also a great way to bring people together. I enjoy spending the time trying to make a dish and seeing what other thinks of it. Typically I just make sides for Thanksgiving or something like a cheesecake during someones birthday. Over time I have learned how to cook several different types of dishes including pasta, fried rice, and steak. Even without any professinal knife skills or actaul culinary experience I enjoy learning new things and improving my skills. Most of the time it feels way better than eating out. Although I have'nt been able to really cook while in college due to the lack of appliances.
