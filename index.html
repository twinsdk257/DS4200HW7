About Me

My name is Sean Kim and I am currently studying Data Science and Environmental and Sustainability Sciences at Northeastern University. One of my favorite hobbies outside of school is cooking. Cooking has always been something that I enjoy because it allows me to be creative while also making something useful. After long days of studying or working on assignments, cooking helps me relax and gives me a break from looking at a computer screen. I enjoy experimenting with different ingredients and trying new recipes that I find online or learn from friends and family.

Cooking is also a great way to bring people together. I enjoy cooking meals for my friends or family when I have the time because it is rewarding to share food with others. Over time I have learned how to cook several different types of dishes including pasta, fried rice, and steak. Even though I am not a professional chef, I enjoy learning new techniques and improving my cooking skills whenever I have the opportunity. This website highlights my interest in cooking and some of the foods I enjoy making.
